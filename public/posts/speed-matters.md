# Why Terminal Speed Matters

Speed is not just about raw performance; it's about the feedback loop. When your tools are fast, you stay in the flow.

## The Flow State
Developing a fast terminal-based workflow has allowed me to focus more on logic and less on waiting for build tools.

## Key Learnings
- Minimalism is a feature.
- Latency is the enemy of productivity.
- Modern tools like Vite and Bun are game changers.
