The landscape of AI has shifted from simple conversational agents to deeply integrated agentic systems.

## Agentic Workflows
We're seeing a move towards AI that can reason, plan, and execute complex tasks autonomously.

## The Future
As we integrate AI more deeply into our development environments, the role of a software engineer is evolving from a coder to an architect of systems.
